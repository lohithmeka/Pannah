The Challenge

Today's educational system with outdated curricula leave our children and teenagers critically unprepared for the future economy.

This systemic failure sparks a devastating crisis: over 65% of educated young Indians are jobless. For those in high-need communities, crushing roadblocks mean even the most resilient often face unemployment.

OUR APPROACH

Panaah forges dual pathways to success
for children and teenagers

Grade 5 - 10

Beyond school, our children become community Solvers, tackling critical issues facing their communities and city. Our Scholars, they prepare for and conquer the most rigorous national and international science and math competitive exams & olympiads.

panaah solvers panaah scholars

Grade 11 - first job

Our teenagers leap beyond college: they build their own startups, create products and services, make films mastering the critical skills needed to thrive in tomorrow's evolving economy. This trajectory sets them up to achieve significant monthly earnings (₹45–50k) by age 21, paving the way for early financial independence.

our impact

In just four years, we’ve grown from 356 to over 850 children, each supported to lead, create, and thrive. These numbers reflect real progress— from learning gains to films, innovations, and community change.

As we look ahead to 2025, we’re excited to see over 750 youth-led projects take root, 180 films spark new conversations, and more teens step into national platforms. The scale is growing—but what moves us most is the depth. Expect even more stories of grit, creativity, and collective power in the months to come.
